# How to Upload Data to Arweave

Arweave is a decentralized storage network designed for permanent data storage. Unlike traditional cloud storage, data uploaded to Arweave is stored permanently across a distributed network of nodes, ensuring your data remains accessible forever. The network uses a unique economic model where you pay once for permanent storage, making it ideal for long-term data preservation and decentralized applications.

## Upload Methods

When it comes to uploading data to Arweave, you have several methods to choose from, each optimized for different use cases and technical skill levels. The best choice depends on your specific needs, whether you're uploading individual files, building applications, or managing large datasets.
