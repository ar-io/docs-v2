Arweave offers **permanent data storage** - pay once, store forever. Choose the method that fits your needs.

## Upload Options Comparison

| Method                | Best For                    | Payment              | Setup    | Control | Reliability         |
| --------------------- | --------------------------- | -------------------- | -------- | ------- | ------------------- |
| **Turbo Bundling**    | Production apps, developers | Multiple options     | Easy SDK | High    | Enterprise-grade    |
| **No-Code (ArDrive)** | Individuals, simple uploads | Credit cards, crypto | None     | Medium  | High                |
| **Direct to Network** | Advanced users, research    | AR tokens only       | Complex  | Maximum | Manual verification |

## Why Choose Turbo?

[Turbo](https://turbo.ardrive.net) is our recommended solution for most applications. It uses the ANS-104 bundling format to provide multiple payment options, optimized performance, and easy SDK integration.

### Key Benefits Over Direct AR Uploads

| Feature                      | Turbo Credits                     | Direct AR Uploads               |
| ---------------------------- | --------------------------------- | ------------------------------- |
| **Per-Byte Pricing**         | ✅ Pay only for data uploaded     | ❌ Pay for empty chunks         |
| **Failed Upload Protection** | ✅ Automatic retry & confirmation | ❌ Manual verification required |
| **Subsidized Uploads**       | ✅ Free uploads under 100 KiB     | ❌ No subsidies available       |
| **Multiple Payment Methods** | ✅ Credit cards, crypto, fiat     | ❌ AR tokens only               |
| **Easy Integration**         | ✅ Simple SDK & CLI               | ❌ Complex protocol knowledge   |
