# How to Upload Data to Arweave

Arweave offers **permanent data storage** - pay once, store forever. Choose the method that fits your needs.

## Getting Started

- [**No-Code Solutions**](/how-to-upload-data/no-code) - Perfect for non-technical users
- [**Bundling Services**](/how-to-upload-data/bundling-services) - Recommended for developers
- [**Direct to Network**](/how-to-upload-data/direct-to-network) - For advanced users

## Why Choose Arweave?

- **Permanent**: Data stored once, available forever
- **Decentralized**: No single point of failure
- **Cost-effective**: Pay once, store forever
- **Censorship-resistant**: No one can take it down

Learn more about the [Arweave protocol](https://arweave.org) and its innovative approach to permanent data storage.

## Quick Start Guide

### **No Code? Start with ArDrive**

The easiest way to get started is with [ArDrive](https://app.ardrive.io) - simply drag and drop your files to upload them permanently to Arweave. Perfect for individuals and teams who want to preserve their data without any technical setup.

→ [Launch ArDrive App](https://app.ardrive.io)

---

### **For Developers: Turbo Bundling Service**

[Turbo](https://turbo.ardrive.net) is our recommended solution for most applications. It uses the ANS-104 bundling format to provide:

- **Multiple payment options** - AR, ETH, SOL, MATIC, or credit cards
- **Optimized performance** - Fast uploads with built-in retry mechanisms
- **Open source** - Run your own bundler or use our scaled service
- **SDK available** - Check out the [Turbo SDK](/sdks/turbo-sdk) for easy integration

→ [Get Started with Turbo](https://turbo.ardrive.net)

---

### **Advanced: Direct to Arweave Network**

For developers requiring full control, you can interact directly with the Arweave protocol. This approach requires:

- Familiarity with the [Arweave protocol](https://arweave.org)
- AR tokens for transaction fees
- Implementation of transaction signing and posting

Best for custom implementations where you need complete control over every aspect of the upload process.

→ [Learn Arweave Protocol](https://arweave.org)

---

## Not Sure Which to Choose?

<Callout type="info">
**Most users should start with ArDrive** for quick uploads or **Turbo** for application development. Direct network access is only needed for specialized use cases.
</Callout>
